# German route research matrix

This is a working inventory, not a finished statement of route signalling. A route remains `pending` until its systems are supported by evidence recorded in `data/germany/routes.json`.

## Research fields

For each route, establish:

- German infrastructure extent and any border transition;
- lineside signalling families;
- subsidiary signals, indicators and boards;
- PZB, LZB, ETCS, GNT/NeiTech and Sifa support;
- train or timetable layers that materially change player guidance;
- repeatable Train Sim World differences or defects;
- representative locations and aspects for screenshots or SVG references.

## First audit group

Start with the Dresden and Saxony routes because they are likely to expose the broadest mix of modern and former Deutsche Reichsbahn signalling:

1. Nahverkehr Dresden (Dresden–Riesa)
2. Tharandter Rampe (Dresden–Chemnitz)
3. Bahnstrecke Leipzig–Dresden
4. Rapid Transit

For each route, the first pass should answer only:

- Which lineside system families are visibly present?
- Which safety systems must the player operate?
- Which signal aspects and boards are demonstrably represented?
- What evidence supports each claim?

Detailed signal pages should follow only after the route inventory prevents accidental omission.
