# Rottenwarnsignale (Ro) — Track-gang warning signals

Track-gang warning signals (*Rottenwarnsignale*) are the warnings given to a permanent-way gang (*Rotte*) working in or beside the track when rail vehicles approach. Three of the four are **acoustic** — sounded on a mixed-tone horn (*Mischklangsignalhorn*) — and tell the staff how to behave: take care, clear the working line, or clear it as fast as possible. The fourth, Ro 4, is a static flag-board that marks the side to clear to.

**Intro — ESO (46) / § 50 (1):** *Rottenwarnsignale geben den im Gleis oder in dessen Nähe beschäftigten Personen Weisungen über ihr Verhalten bei Annäherung von Fahrzeugen.* — "Track-gang warning signals give the persons working in the track or near it instructions on how to behave when vehicles approach." Per § 50 (2) the signals are given with the mixed-tone signal horn (*Mischklangsignalhorn*); they are to be obeyed even when heard in only one of the two pitches.
