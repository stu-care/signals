# Haupt- und Vorsignalverbindungen (Sv) вҖ” Combined main/distant signals (S-Bahn)

> DBAG Signalbuch (SB) DS/DV 301, 2006 вҖ” chapter **Haupt- und Vorsignalverbindungen (Sv)**. Source pages: SB-DBAG 2006 pp.18вҖ“20.

A combined main-and-distant signal (Sv) is a special S-Bahn light signal that unites a main signal and a distant signal side by side on one screen. The **left** lights give the main-signal aspect (Hp 0/1/2) for the block section ahead; the **right** lights give the distant-signal aspect (Vr 0/1/2) for the next Sv signal.

**Intro вҖ” ESO (20) / В§ 6 (1).** *Haupt- und Vorsignalverbindungen sind nur bei Stadtschnellbahnen auf eigenem BahnkГ¶rper (S-Bahnen) vorhanden. Eine Haupt- und Vorsignalverbindung ist ein Lichtsignal besonderer Art, das Haupt- und Vorsignal auf einem Signalschirm nebeneinander vereinigt. Die linken Lichter entsprechen den Hauptsignalbildern В»Hp 0В«, В»Hp 1В« oder В»Hp 2В« und geben an, ob der anschlieГҹende Gleisabschnitt von einem Zug befahren werden darf. Die rechten Lichter entsprechen den Vorsignalbildern В»Vr 0В«, В»Vr 1В« oder В»Vr 2В« zu dem am nГӨchsten Sv-Signal leuchtenden Hauptsignalbild.*

Combined main-and-distant signals exist only on rapid-transit railways on their own trackbed (S-Bahnen). Such a signal is a special kind of light signal that unites a main signal and a distant signal side by side on one signal screen. The **left-hand lights** correspond to the main-signal aspects "Hp 0", "Hp 1" or "Hp 2" and indicate whether the block section ahead may be entered by a train. The **right-hand lights** correspond to the distant-signal aspects "Vr 0", "Vr 1" or "Vr 2" for the main-signal aspect lit at the next Sv signal.

**Placement вҖ” AB 31 / В§ 6 (2).** The signals may stand to the right or left of, or above, the track.

**Repeaters вҖ” AB 32 / В§ 6 (2a).** Where the sight of an Sv signal is obstructed, the lights corresponding to the distant-signal aspects may be repeated (distant-signal repeaters). Such signals are then identified by a white additional light arranged on the left.

**Shortened braking distance вҖ” AB 33 / В§ 6 (3).** Signals (except "Sv 0") that stand at more than 5 % less than the required braking distance ahead of the following signal are identified by a white-lit downward-pointing arrow above the signal aspects.
